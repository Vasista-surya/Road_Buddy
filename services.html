Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a bike pool match?

+

Finding a bike pool match is simple. Enter your starting point, destination, and preferred time in our app. Our algorithm will show you available matches based on your route and schedule. You can view rider profiles, ratings, and choose the best match for your journey.

Is bike pooling safe?

+

Safety is our top priority. All users undergo identity verification, and we have a comprehensive rating system. Our app includes real-time tracking, emergency assistance, and a trusted community of verified users. You can also view detailed profiles and ratings before accepting a ride.

How does payment work?

+

We offer multiple secure payment options. The fare is calculated based on distance and displayed before confirming the ride. Payments can be made through the app using credit/debit cards, digital wallets, or UPI. For regular rides, you can also set up automatic payments to streamline the process.

Can I schedule rides in advance?

+

Yes, you can schedule rides in advance. Regular users can schedule rides up to 3 days ahead, while Premium users can book up to 7 days in advance. This feature is particularly useful for regular commuters who want to secure their rides for the entire work week.

What if my ride gets canceled?

+

If your ride gets canceled, our system immediately starts looking for alternative matches. You'll receive notifications about new potential matches. If no suitable alternative is found, any payment made will be refunded to your account. Premium users get priority in finding replacement rides.
