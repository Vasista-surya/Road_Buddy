How do I create an account?

+

Creating an account is simple. Download our app or visit our website, click on "Sign Up," and follow the instructions. You'll need to provide your name, email address, phone number, and create a password. You'll also need to verify your email and phone number for security purposes.

How do I reset my password?

+

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click on the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password.

How do I book a ride?

+

To book a ride, open the app and enter your pickup location and destination. Choose the "Find Ride" option, and you'll see available rides matching your route. Browse through the options, check rider profiles and ratings, and select the ride that suits you best. Confirm your booking by making the payment.

Can I schedule a ride in advance?

+

Yes, you can schedule rides in advance. When searching for a ride, select the "Schedule" option and choose your preferred date and time. Regular users can schedule rides up to 3 days ahead, while Premium users can book up to 7 days in advance.

What payment methods are accepted?

+

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, digital wallets like Paytm and Google Pay, and net banking. You can add your preferred payment methods in the "Payment" section of your profile.

How do refunds work?

+

If you cancel a ride more than 24 hours before the scheduled time, you'll receive a full refund excluding the service fee. For cancellations within 24 hours, a partial refund will be issued based on our cancellation policy. If the ride provider cancels, you'll receive a full refund including the service fee.

How does Road Buddy ensure safety?

+

We prioritize safety through multiple measures including identity verification, profile ratings, real-time ride tracking, and an emergency SOS button. All users undergo a verification process, and we maintain a community rating system to ensure only trusted individuals use our platform.

What should I do in case of an emergency during a ride?

+

In case of an emergency, use the SOS button in the app which will alert our emergency response team and share your real-time location. You can also directly call emergency services through the app. We recommend sharing your ride details with a trusted contact before starting your journey.

How do I become a ride provider?

+

To become a ride provider, go to the "Offer Ride" section in the app. You'll need to complete additional verification steps including vehicle registration, driving license verification, and insurance details. Once approved, you can start offering rides on your regular routes.

Is Road Buddy available in my city?

+

Road Buddy is currently available in major cities across India including Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, and Kolkata. We're rapidly expanding to other cities. Check the app or website for the most up-to-date information on service availability in your area.
