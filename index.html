Help Center

How do I book a Bikepool ride?

+

You can book a Bikepool ride on our mobile app, or on RoadBuddy.in. Just type in your destination, select the date you would like to travel and select the Bikepool that best suits you! Some rides can be booked immediately, and other rides are manually approved by the driver. Either way, booking a Bikepool ride is quick, easy and convenient.

How do I publish a Bikepool ride?

+

Selling a Bikepool ride on RoadBuddy is simple. To put up your ride, use our mobile app or RoadBuddy.in. Indicate your departure and arrival points, the date and time of your departure how many passengers you can take and the price per seat. You'll also need to choose how you want to accept bookings (either automatically or manually), and you have the option of adding any important details you think your passengers should know about. Then tap 'Publish ride' and you're done!

How do I cancel my Bikepool ride?

+

If you need to change your plans, you can always cancel your Bikepool ride from the 'Your rides' page of our app. The sooner you cancel, the better. That way the driver has time to accept new passengers. The amount of your refund will be based on how early you cancel. If you cancel more than 24 hours before departure, for example, you'll receive a full refund, excluding the service fee.

What are the benefits of travelling by Bikepool?

+

There are multiple advantages to Bikepooling, over other means of transport. Travelling by Bikepool is usually more affordable, especially for longer distances. Bikepooling is also more eco-friendly, as sharing a Bike means there will be fewer Bikes on the road, and therefore fewer emissions. Taking a Bikepool ride is also a safe method of transport during these modern days.

How much does a Bikepool ride cost?

+

The fares of a Bikepool trip may differ significantly, and are based on factors such as distance, departure time, the demand of that ride and beyond. It is also the responsibility of the person driving to determine how much to charge per seat, so it's difficult to put a precise price tag on a ride. Look at some of our top Bikepool destinations to get an idea of price, or start searching for your next Bikepool ride on RoadBuddy.in.

How do I start Bikepooling?

+

Bikepooling with RoadBuddy is really easy, and for free! Just register for an account and inform us of some basic information about yourself. After you have an account with RoadBuddy, you are able to book or post rides directly on our website or app.
