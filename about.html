About Road Buddy

Revolutionizing the way people commute

Our Mission

At Road Buddy, our mission is to transform urban commuting through innovative bike pooling solutions. We aim to reduce traffic congestion, lower carbon emissions, and create a community of responsible commuters who share resources for a sustainable future.

Founded in 2022, Road Buddy has quickly grown to become the leading bike pooling platform in the region, connecting thousands of riders daily and saving countless hours of commute time.
