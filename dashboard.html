How to Use Road Buddy

Finding a Ride

  1. 1

    Navigate to Find Ride

    Click on the "Find Ride" tab in the main navigation.

  2. 2

    Enter Your Details

    Enter your origin, destination, and travel date.

  3. 3

    Browse Available Rides

    Browse through the list of available rides that match your criteria.

  4. 4

    Book Your Ride

    Click "Book Now" on the ride that suits you best.

Posting a Ride

  1. 1

    Navigate to Post Ride

    Click on the "Post Ride" tab in the main navigation.

  2. 2

    Enter Route Details

    Enter your origin, destination, date, and time.

  3. 3

    Add Vehicle Information

    Select your vehicle type and enter details.

  4. 4

    Set Price and Preferences

    Set your price per seat and any preferences.

Safety Tips

  • Always verify the identity of your ride partner
  • Share your ride details with a friend or family member
  • Use the in-app messaging to communicate
  • Use the emergency button if you feel unsafe
